How to Play

Objective

Test your reaction time by tapping as quickly as possible when the screen changes color.

Instructions

  • Tap the screen to start a practice round
  • After a countdown, wait for the screen to turn green
  • Tap as quickly as possible when the screen turns green
  • Complete 5 trials to get your results
  • Don't tap too early or you'll have to restart the trial

Controls

  • Tap anywhere on the screen to interact
  • Press Space or Enter to interact (keyboard support)
  • Use the "History" button to view past results

About

The average human reaction time is between 200-300ms. Professional gamers can achieve reaction times under 150ms.
